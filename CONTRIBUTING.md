# 🤝 Contributing to VakyaLang

Thank you for your interest in contributing to VakyaLang (वाक्). We welcome contributions that align with our vision of reviving Sanskrit as a functional computing substrate.

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the Repository:** Create your own copy of the repo.
2. **Create a Feature Branch:** `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`
3. **Write and Test:** Ensure your code follows existing patterns and includes tests in `tests/`.
4. **Submit a Pull Request:** Provide a clear explanation of your changes.

## Guiding Principles
- **Sanskrit Integrity:** Keywords and built-in names should be authentic Sanskrit words.
- **Pāṇinian Rigor:** Maintain the formal, rule-based approach to language grammar.
- **Nyāya Logic:** Align reasoning systems with established Sanskrit logical schools.
- **Radical Honesty:** Ensure implementations are functional, not just "skins."
